Sampling method

MusicBrainz has no random endpoint. The app chooses several years across the selected range, fetches a random 40-item page within the first 1,200 results for each year, and shuffles the candidates. It then finds exact YouTube Music matches and applies the minimum-play filter. This is not a uniform sample of either catalog.
